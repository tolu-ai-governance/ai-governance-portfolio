# AI Governance Portfolio
**Tolu Owadokun | PhD | AI Strategy & Governance**

This portfolio contains applied AI governance work demonstrating 
NIST AI RMF-aligned risk assessment methodology across healthcare, 
enterprise, and consulting contexts.

## Portfolio Documents

### 1. AI Risk Assessment — LLM Patient Education (Healthcare)
Risk assessment for deploying an LLM-powered patient education system 
in a regional community health system. Covers GOVERN, MAP, MEASURE, 
and MANAGE functions with clinical safety findings and deployment 
recommendations.

### 2. AI Risk Assessment — Enterprise LLM Copilot (Generic)
Risk assessment for enterprise-wide deployment of an LLM productivity 
copilot across a large multi-industry organization. Covers data 
leakage, shadow AI, hallucination risk, and governance program build.

### 3. AI Risk Assessment — Consulting Firm LLM (Professional Services)
Risk assessment for LLM integration in client engagement delivery at 
a management consulting firm. Covers client data isolation, NDA 
compliance, and AI quality governance in billable work.

## Framework
All assessments apply the NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0)
